ER diagram to Relational database schema Mapping Algorithm
2026-03-30 14:02   label UoA, Database

Step1: Mapping of Regular Entity Types

for any simple entity, include its simple attributes and its primary key

Step2: Mapping of Weak Entity Types

includes its simple attributes and foreign keys it related to, combine its partial key and the foreign key as a primary key.

Step3: Mapping of Binary 1:1 Relationship Types

“absorb” the 1:1 relationship into one of the table by foreign key, and then include any simple attributes of the 1:1 relation type as attributes of the relation which absorded the other one.

Step4: Mapping of Binary 1:N Relationship Types

include the primary key of the 1-side relation to the N-side relation as a foreign key, and then include any simple attributes of the 1:N relation type as attributes of the N-side relation

Step5: Mapping of Binary M:N Relationship Types

create a new relation by taking the primary keys for the related relations as foreign keys, and they are combined as a primary key, and then include any simple attributes of this relationship as attributes

Step6: Mapping of Multivalued attributes

create a new relation for those multivated attribute, includes the primary key of the origin relation as a foreign key and combined as a primary key

Ste7: Mapping of N-ary Relationship Types

create a new relation for those N-ary relationship, includes the primary key of those related relation as a foreign key and combined as a primary key, and then include any simple attributes of this relationship as attributes
